Governance :

The governance program cuts across areas where Sneha Abhiyan works. It promotes citizen and community (especially the women,Olderpeople,PWD, and Dalit) participation, transparent and accountable public governance, improved public service delivery, and social accountability through productive engagement with government, networking and policy dialogue.

We Collaborated With

Sneha Abhiyan "a campaign of love" was a response to the Super Cyclone 1999 and ensured community-based rehabilitation of the most vulnerable among the survivors of the Orissa super cyclone - children,women,PWDs and old people who are left without the care of families.
